Flexboxe'd, HTML5-tagged & mobile friendly!

Damn, CSS is tricky...

So i just managed to solve a irritating bug with the site, where I could not get the footer to properly stick to the bottom of the page.
My solution was:

  1. Use the somewhat standard "sticky footer" implementation. You wrap everything above the footer in a "wrapper" div. This includes the header. The wrapper should have a min-height of calc(100vh - [footer pixel height]).
    You can find the height by either specifially setting it (watch your paddings and margins), or by inspecting it with your browsers developer tools.
    Consult CSS-Tricks guide for a propper solution that works across older browsers as well.
  2. Remove the vertical margins on the footer content! Use padding intead.
    This was the mistake I made that made sticking the footer to the bottom much more difficult than it had to be.
  3. Prepare to get screwed by CSS. Inspect everything with the developer tools of choice and don't give up!
